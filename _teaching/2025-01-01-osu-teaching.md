Served as a Teaching Assistant for **FE 440/550**, a course focusing on the analysis and optimization of forest operations. My role involved supporting students in mastering technical logging systems, cost analysis, and the application of engineering principles to forest harvest planning and transportation.

### Course Focus
The course emphasizes the quantitative analysis of forest operations, including productivity modeling, harvest system selection, and the economic evaluation of forest engineering projects.
