Served as a Teaching Assistant for **FOR 462**, a core course focusing on the application of economic principles to forest management. My responsibilities included assisting students with economic modeling, grading technical assignments, and supporting the instruction of forest valuation and decision-making processes.

### Course Description
This course covers the economic and social factors affecting forest resource use. It includes topics such as capital theory, timber production economics, and the evaluation of non-market forest outputs.
