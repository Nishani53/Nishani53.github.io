Presented at the **IUFRO World Congress 2024** in Stockholm, this research analyzes the localized economic impacts of establishing a mass timber industry in Michigan. The study utilizes regional modeling to demonstrate how mass timber production can stimulate job growth and industrial output within the forest products sector.
