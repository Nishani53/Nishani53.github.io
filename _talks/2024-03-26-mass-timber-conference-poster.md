Presented at the **International Mass Timber Conference 2024** in Portland, this research evaluates the financial and industrial benefits of converting small-diameter timber—often a byproduct of wildfire restoration—into high-value mass timber products. The study covers regional economic impacts across both Oregon and Washington.
